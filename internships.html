If you're a student passionate about shaping the future of distributed computing in an academic setting, the Center for High Throughput Computing (CHTC) at the University of Wisconsin-Madison (UW) invites you to be a part of our vibrant internship program. Situated within the esteemed UW Department of Computer Sciences, frequently ranked among the top ten in the nation, our internships offer a glimpse into the world of high-throughput distributed computing, under the guidance of accomplished faculty and professionals.

Student Hourly Positions (Undergrad and Grad)

We're always looking for smart motivated students to partner with software developer and system administrator mentors. We expect students to work between 10 and 20 hours a week, with 10 of those being during business hours, with some flexibility on remaining hours. During the summer and breaks, it is possible to work up to 40 hours per week.
