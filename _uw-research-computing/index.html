Who We Are

We are the University of Wisconsin-Madison's core computational resource provider for large scale computing. UW-Madison staff, students, faculty, and external collaborators are welcome to use CHTC resources to carry out their computationally-intensive research.
CHTC provides a variety of resources and tools to meet the demands of the University's research community.
We provide no-cost compute resources (CPUs/GPUs, high-memory servers, etc.), as well as no-cost personalized consultations and classroom support.
