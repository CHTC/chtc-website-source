## Who is eligible for a CHTC account?

To apply for a CHTC account, you must:

1. Be a **UW-Madison affiliate** with an active NetID (faculty, staff, graduate, undergraduate, or approved [external collaborator](#external-collaborators)).
2. Use CHTC resources *only* for **research** or **approved courses**.
3. Have a **faculty sponsor**. This is typically the Principal Investigator (PI) of your research group or your course instructor.

> ### Don't have a NetID yet?
{:.tip-header}
> 1. Your faculty sponsor should follow the instructions below in the [External Collaborators](#external-collaborators) section.
> 1. We will review the request and send you an invitation to create a NetID.
> 1. Once your NetID is ready, fill out the [Account Request Form](https://userapp.chtc.wisc.edu/forms/user-applications/create/)!
{:.tip}

## External collaborators

UW-Madison faculty members can sponsor accounts for external collaborators (including graduating students) for up to one year. Access may be renewed.

**Only the sponsoring faculty members** should fill out our external collaborator form:

## Use CHTC for a course

Do you need computing for your class? We can provide class accounts and support your class with guest presentations. Instructors should [email us](mailto:chtc@cs.wisc.edu) to inquire about how we can support your class!
