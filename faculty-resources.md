## For faculty hiring committees

CHTC staff are happy to meet with faculty candidates at any point in the recruitment 
process - during their campus visit, during the offer negotiation process, or once 
they are ready to start at UW - Madison. In these meetings, we can discuss computing 
needs with prospective faculty, introduce CHTC capabilities, and discuss the possibility 
of prioritized resources. **We strongly recommend introducing CHTC 
to the recruitment process sooner rather than later** -- early communication means 
that we can lay the groundwork that will allow new faculty to hit the ground running. 

### Resources

* [Set up a meeting between CHTC and potential faculty](mailto:chtc-faculty-recruiting@g-groups.wisc.edu) 
* [One-pager: summary of CHTC services](to-do) (Only available via UW - Madison NetID)

## For prospective or incoming faculty

To get started at CHTC, we recommend scheduling a consultation with our staff. 
As above, the sooner you talk to us, the sooner we can connect you with resources 
at CHTC or elsewhere to succeed in your work at UW - Madison. 

### Resources

* [Meet with us about your startup proposal or computing needs](mailto:chtc-faculty-support@g-groups.wisc.edu)
* [One-pager: summary of CHTC services](to-do) (Only available via UW - Madison NetID)
* [One-pager: getting started at CHTC](to-do) (Only available via UW - Madison NetID)
* [Request an account](https://chtc.cs.wisc.edu/uw-research-computing/form.html)

## For current faculty

If your group is new to CHTC, we recommend scheduling a meeting with us, and having your 
group members join. After this meeting, we can create accounts on our systems 
and provide next steps for getting started. 

If your group is currently using CHTC, we want to partner with you in improving your 
CHTC experience. The best way to do this is to contact us; we'll set up a meeting or call to 
discuss any roadblocks or challenges you are experiencing, and arrange a follow-up 
plan to address any issues.
